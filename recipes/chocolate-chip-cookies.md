## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 cups chocolate chips
- 1 pinch sea salt flakes

## Instructions

1. **Brown the Butter:** Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Swirl frequently. The butter will melt, then foam, then crackle. After 3 to 5 minutes, it will quiet down and brown specks will begin to form on the bottom. Once it smells nutty and turns golden-brown, immediately pour it into a mixing bowl to cool for 10 minutes.
2. **Cream the Fats and Sugars:** Add the granulated sugar and brown sugar to the cooled browned butter. Whisk vigorously for 1 minute until fully combined and smooth.
3. **Add Wet Ingredients:** Whisk in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Beat the mixture for 2 minutes until it becomes slightly pale and thick.
4. **Whisk Dry Ingredients:** In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and 1 tsp salt.
5. **Combine Wet and Dry:** Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients. Fold gently with a spatula just until the flour disappears. Do not overmix!
6. **Fold in Chocolate:** Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the cookie dough.
7. **Chill the Dough:** Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour (24 hours is even better for maximum flavor!).
8. **Preheat and Prepare:** Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
9. **Scoop and Bake:** Scoop large balls of dough (about 3 tbsp each) and place them on the baking sheets, leaving 2 inches of space between cookies. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden-brown but the centers still look slightly soft.
10. **Cool and Finish:** Remove from the oven and immediately sprinkle the tops with a pinch of flaky sea salt. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
