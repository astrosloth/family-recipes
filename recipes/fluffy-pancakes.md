## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 eggs
- 4 tbsp butter, melted
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. **Sour the Milk:** Whisk the milk and vinegar together in a bowl. Let it sit for 5 minutes to sour (creating an instant buttermilk). Alternatively, just use 2 cups of fresh buttermilk.
2. **Combine Dry Ingredients:** In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. **Combine Wet Ingredients:** Whisk the eggs and melted butter into the soured milk mixture.
4. **Mix the Batter:** Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Whisk gently just until combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy and thick—do not overmix, or the pancakes will become tough! Let the batter rest for 5 minutes; you will see tiny bubbles forming on the surface.
5. **Preheat the Griddle:** Heat a large non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly brush the surface with a small amount of vegetable oil.
6. **Pour and Cook:** Pour 1/3 cup of batter onto the hot griddle for each pancake. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes. You will know it is time to flip when the edges look dry and bubbles pop and stay open on the surface of the pancake.
7. **Flip and Finish:** Flip the pancake carefully and cook the other side for an additional 2 minutes, or until cooked through and golden-brown.
8. **Keep Warm and Serve:** Transfer cooked pancakes to a warm plate and cover with a clean kitchen towel to keep warm while you cook the remaining batter. Serve warm piled high with butter and maple syrup!
