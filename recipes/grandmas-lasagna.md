## Ingredients

- 12 lasagna noodles
- 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
- 3/4 lb lean ground beef
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans (14.5 oz each) crushed tomatoes
- 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
- 2 cans (6.5 oz each) tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp dried basil
- 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
- 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- 16 oz ricotta cheese
- 1 egg
- 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced or shredded
- 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. **Prep the Meat Sauce:** In a large Dutch oven or pot over medium heat, cook the sweet Italian sausage, ground beef, chopped onion, and minced garlic until browned and crumbled. Drain any excess fat.
2. **Simmer the Sauce:** Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with the sugar, dried basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tsp salt, ground black pepper, and 2 tbsp fresh parsley. Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
3. **Cook the Lasagna Noodles:** Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Boil the lasagna noodles for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
4. **Prepare the Cheese Mixture:** In a medium mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, remaining 2 tbsp fresh parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt. Mix thoroughly until smooth.
5. **Preheat the Oven:** Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
6. **Assemble the Lasagna Layers:** In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce in the bottom. Arrange 6 lasagna noodles lengthwise over the sauce. Spread one-half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with one-third of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
7. **Repeat the Layers:** Repeat the layers: remaining ricotta mixture, another one-third of the mozzarella, 1 1/2 cups of sauce, and 1/4 cup Parmesan. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
8. **Bake the Lasagna:** Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (tent it slightly so it doesn't touch the cheese). Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake uncovered for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
9. **Rest and Serve:** Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving. This lets the layers set beautifully.
